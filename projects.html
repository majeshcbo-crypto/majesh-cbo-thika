Projects & Activities

Majesh CBO Thika runs several community projects throughout the year. Below are some of our key activities.

Tree Planting
We organize tree-planting events to improve our local environment.
Community Clean-ups
Clean-up drives to keep Thika Majengo tidy and healthy.
Youth Workshops
Entrepreneurship and skills training for the youth.
Health Camps
Medical outreach and hygiene education programs.
Road Safety
Awareness campaigns and collaborations with local authorities.
